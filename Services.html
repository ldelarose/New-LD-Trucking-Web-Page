Trucking Services

Copeland Trucking is an Oklahoma City based trucking company that specializes in the following services:

  • Heavy Hauling: single drops, double drops, flat beds
  • Freight Management & Logistic Solutions
  • Forklift and Crane Service
  • Outside Storage
  • Trailer Hostling
  • Round-the-clock trucking service
  • Air Ride Van services

With over 14 acres of land, we can also provide storage for shipments and loads large and small.
Copeland is a member of the Oklahoma Trucking Association
